# CHAMPIONSHIP ARENA
## A Cutthroat Game of Skill, Stunts, and Showmanship
*Hamsters & Monsters — One Deck. Six Rings. One Champion.*
*For 2–4 Players • 20–30 Minutes • Ages 10+*

---

## The Premise

Six factions of elite hamsters compete in the Grand Championship Arena — a televised gauntlet where only the most daring, clever, and ruthless win the crown.

Each round, three Competition Rings open. Players secretly commit their best efforts, then reveal simultaneously. The highest Energy Score claims the Ring — but each Ring has its own twisted logic. Sometimes you score lowest to win. Sometimes only the extreme stunts count. Sometimes raw charisma wins over precision.

The Superstar Talents amplify your performance. The Stunt Double lets you swap tricks mid-performance. The Crowd is watching — and it has opinions. Spectators drop in uninvited, cheering favorites, issuing challenges, and scrambling everyone's plans. The Fan Favorite declares a Double Points round, doubling all stakes. The Crowd goes wild.

Welcome to the Arena. The whistle blows. Give them a show.

---

## WHAT YOU NEED

- **66-card Hamsters & Monsters deck** (6 factions × ranks 0–10)
- **4 dice per player** (d6) — your Energy Dice
- **6 Ring Tiles** (one per faction — Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Purple)
- **3 Ring tokens** per player (to mark which Rings you're competing in)
- **Score track:** 0–15 Fan Points (FP)
- **The Stunt Pool:** 6 cards dealt face-up from the deck (the market)
- **The Spectator Deck:** 12 Spectator Cards (see page 7) — shuffle before each game

---

## THE SIX RINGS

Each Competition Ring is tied to a faction and has a unique scoring twist:

| Ring | Faction | Twist |
|------|---------|-------|
| 🟥 **Red Ring** — Speed Sprint | Super-Dupes | **Highest Energy wins** — raw dice power, cards ignored |
| 🟧 **Orange Ring** — Finders Challenge | Finders-Keepers | **Most Cards wins** — quantity beats quality |
| 🟨 **Yellow Ring** — Trick Showcase | Tinkerers | **Only Extremes** — only 0s and 10s score; all other ranks = 0 |
| 🟩 **Green Ring** — Prediction Station | Prognosticators | **Odd Doubles** — odd dice values are doubled |
| 🟦 **Blue Ring** — Illusion Arena | Magicians | **Even Doubles** — even card ranks are doubled |
| 🟪 **Purple Ring** — Time Warp | Time Travelers | **Lowest Energy wins** — reverse the rules, score from behind |

**Important:** Ring powers apply BEFORE Talents.

---

## THE SPECTATOR DECK

The arena is packed. The crowd is chaotic. The Spectator Deck captures the energy of an enthusiastic audience — they cheer, they jeer, they demand more.

**Format:** 12 small cards (or a reference sheet). Shuffle before each game.

**How it works:**
- During setup, place the Spectator Deck face-down beside the arena.
- At the **START of each round**, the round's player draws the top Spectator card and reads it aloud. The effect resolves immediately — **before** anyone rolls or commits.
- After resolving, discard the Spectator card. The next round, draw the next one.
- If the Spectator Deck runs out, shuffle the discards and continue.
- **Spectator cards can target anyone**, including the player who drew them. The crowd is unpredictable.

### SPECTATOR CARD EFFECTS

| # | Card Name | Effect |
|---|-----------|--------|
| 1 | **🎉 The Roaring Crowd** | Double Points this round! All Ring wins score double FP (2/6/12 instead of 1/3/6). The stakes just got higher. |
| 2 | **😱 The Jittery Hamster** | Challenge at the **Red Ring**: highest *single die* wins a bonus **+1 FP**. If tied, no bonus. |
| 3 | **🎭 The Dramatic Ref** | The player with the **most cards in hand** must reveal one face-down Stunt Double from any opponent's pile. That opponent may respond by revealing theirs. |
| 4 | **🌟 The Starstruck Fan** | Choose one player. That player draws **3 cards** and their next Ring win this round is worth **+2 bonus FP**. |
| 5 | **😤 The Jeering Rival** | Choose a player who did NOT win a Ring last round. That player must **discard 2 cards** from their hand face-down (drawn from randomly). |
| 6 | **🔥 The Underdog Cheer** | The player with the **fewest FP** gets **+1 die** this round (roll 5 dice instead of 4, but must still follow max 2/Ring rule). |
| 7 | **🎪 The Wild Card Toss** | Dump the top **3 cards** of the deck into the Stunt Pool immediately. Then refill to 6 cards. |
| 8 | **🃏 The Card Shark** | Choose a Ring. All players swap one of their face-down cards at that Ring with another player of your choice. (The ref handles swaps.) |
| 9 | **👑 The Champion's Welcome** | The player with the **most Trophy cards** (face-up cards on won Rings) draws **2 cards**. If tied, all tied players draw 2. |
| 10 | **💥 The Chaos Round** | **No dice this round!** All dice values are 0. Only card ranks count. Strategy reset — who built the best hand? |
| 11 | **🤝 The Peace Offering** | All players simultaneously reveal one face-down Stunt Double (if they have one). Everyone who reveals draws **1 card**. No other effects. |
| 12 | **🌈 The Rainbow Ring** | Choose one Ring. This round, it is **Wild** — all cards played there count as rank 10, and dice count at face value. (Yes, even Purple's lowest-wins is overridden.) |

## Setup (2 minutes)

1. **Shuffle** the deck and place it face-down as the draw pile.
2. **Deal 5 cards** face-down to each player. Keep your hand secret.
3. **Draft one Talent** (see below) — keep it secret until you use it.
4. **Place 3 Ring tiles** face-up in the center. These are the active Rings for Round 1.
5. **Deal 6 cards** face-up next to the rings. This is the **Stunt Pool** (your market).
6. **Shuffle the Spectator Deck** and place it beside the arena. Draw the first Spectator card — resolve it before the first round begins.
7. **Roll for First:** Highest total dice roll goes first.

---

## YOUR TURN — SIX STEPS

> **New Step 0 — SPECTATOR INTERLUDE** is added before the existing five steps.

### Step 0 — SPECTATOR INTERLUDE (Before Rolling)
The round's starting player draws the top Spectator card from the deck and reads it aloud. The effect resolves **immediately** before any player rolls or commits. All players are affected equally.

### Step 1 — THE ROLL
Roll your **4 Energy Dice**. These are your power for the round.

> *Note: If the Spectator card "The Chaos Round" is in effect, all dice values are 0 this round.*

### Step 2 — THE COMMIT (Simultaneous)
All players commit at the same time. No talking during Commit.

**Choose Your Rings:**
- Assign your 4 dice to the 3 active Rings
- Maximum **2 dice per Ring**
- You must place dice on **at least 2 different Rings**
- All dice assignments are **OPEN** — everyone sees who's competing where

**Play Your Stunt Card:**
- Choose **1 card** from your hand and place it **face-down** on one of your assigned Rings
- You may also place **1 card face-down** on your **Stunt Double pile** (your hidden reserve)
- The Stunt Double can be revealed and moved to any Ring during Resolution

> *Note: If the Spectator card "The Wild Card Toss" was drawn this round, the Stunt Pool has been expanded before commitment begins.*

### Step 3 — THE REVEAL
When all players say "Ready," flip all face-down cards simultaneously.

### Step 4 — CALCULATE ENERGY SCORE

For each Ring, add up total Energy:

```
Energy Score = Sum of dice values at that Ring + Sum of card ranks at that Ring
```

Then apply the **Ring Power** (see Ring reference above):

| Ring | Power | Effect |
|------|-------|--------|
| Red | Speed Sprint | Only dice count — card ranks are ignored |
| Orange | Finders Challenge | Only card count matters — most cards played wins |
| Yellow | Trick Showcase | Only 0s and 10s count; all other ranks = 0 |
| Green | Prediction Station | Odd dice values are doubled |
| Blue | Illusion Arena | Even card ranks are doubled |
| Purple | Time Warp | **Lowest total wins** (dice + cards, after doubling) |

> *Note: If the Spectator card "The Rainbow Ring" was drawn this round, that Ring treats all cards as rank 10 and dice at face value.*

Then apply your **Talent effects** (see below).

> *Note: If the Spectator card "The Chaos Round" was drawn, all dice = 0, so only card ranks score. If "The Roaring Crowd" was drawn, all FP from this round is doubled (see Step 5).*

**The Stunt Double:** If you placed a Stunt Double, you may reveal it and move it to **any Ring** before calculating scores. This is your trump card.

### Step 5 — CLAIM AND DRAFT

**Claim the Ring:**
- Highest Energy Score claims the Ring → **1 Fan Point**
- If you claim **2 Rings** in one round → **3 Fan Points**
- If you claim **all 3 Rings** → **6 Fan Points** (Sweep!)

> *If "The Roaring Crowd" Spectator card is active, these become 2/6/12 FP respectively.*

**Place your Trophy:** The winner places **one card from their hand face-up** on the Ring as a Trophy. This card stays there for future rounds — it counts toward your hand size but not toward scoring.

**Draw Cards:** Winner draws cards equal to **2 + number of Purple (Time Traveler) cards you played this round**.

**Spectator Bonus FP:** If the Spectator card "The Jittery Hamster" was drawn, the player with the highest single die at Red Ring gains **+1 FP** (in addition to any Ring win). This is awarded after all Ring claims.

**Spectator Draw Bonus:** If the Spectator card "The Starstruck Fan" was drawn and the chosen player wins any Ring this round, they gain **+2 bonus FP** (in addition to their Ring win FP). If the chosen player wins no Ring, the bonus is lost.

**Draft from the Stunt Pool:** After resolving, you may claim **one card** from the Stunt Pool by paying its rank value with your dice (e.g., roll a 7, claim the rank-7 card). You may draft **once per round**. Unclaimed cards stay.

## TALENT CARDS (Draft One at Game Start)

Each player secretly holds one Talent for the entire game. Reveal when you use it (unless stated otherwise).

| Talent | Name | Effect | When |
|--------|------|--------|------|
| 🔴 **The Showman** | Double Points | This round: Score **double FP** (2/6/12). If you win 0 Rings, **lose 2 FP**. | Before rolling |
| 🟪 **The Time Traveler** | Rewind | After reveal, **swap one of your cards** with an opponent's card at the same Ring. | During resolution |
| 🟧 **The Collector** | Finders-Keepers | When you claim a Ring, draw **+2 cards** instead of the standard draw. | Passive |
| 🟥 **The Sprinter** | Speed Surge | All your dice are **+2** this round. | Before rolling |
| 🟩 **The Analyst** | Probability | Look at all face-down cards at **one Ring** before they are revealed. | Before resolution |
| 🟦 **The Illusionist** | Misdirection | Choose **one Ring** to be Wild this round — any suit counts as any suit there. | Before Ring selection |

---

## THE STUNT POOL (DRAFTING MARKET)

After claiming, players may draft from the **Stunt Pool**:
- Pay **exact die value** to claim a card of matching rank
- Example: You rolled a 7 — you may claim the rank-7 card from the Stunt Pool
- You may draft **once per round**
- If no exact match, you may not draft

**Stunt Pool Flush:** If you fail to claim any Ring AND fail to draft from the Stunt Pool, you may **flush the Pool** — discard all remaining Stunt Pool cards and deal 6 new ones.

**Spectator Interference:** If the Spectator card "The Wild Card Toss" is drawn during Step 0, immediately dump the top 3 cards of the deck into the Stunt Pool before anyone drafts. Then refill to 6 cards. The pool is temporarily 9 cards.

---

## SCORING

- Win 1 Ring = **1 FP** *(2 FP if "The Roaring Crowd" is active)*
- Win 2 Rings = **3 FP** *(6 FP if "The Roaring Crowd" is active)*
- Win 3 Rings = **6 FP** *(12 FP if "The Roaring Crowd" is active)*

**Sweep Bonus:** Winning all 3 Rings in one round is a **Sweep!** — place your Trophy on each Ring AND draw 3 cards.

**First to 15 FP wins immediately.** (The 15 FP cap is your safety valve — the game can't spiral forever.)

---

## TWO-PLAYER VARIANT

Each player controls **two Talents** (draft both at start). You are effectively playing as a "team of two hamsters." All other rules apply. In the Two-Player variant, the Spectator Deck is shuffled once at game start and placed beside the arena — draw one card per round as normal.

---

## SOLO MODE — BEAT THE CHAMPION

Play against **The Champion AI**:

1. You control 2 Talents (choose both at start)
2. The Champion rolls 3 dice and assigns them randomly to 2 Rings
3. The Champion plays 1 random card to each Ring it has dice in
4. The Champion has a **Stunt Double** (face-down, revealed only if the Champion loses a Ring where it had a Stunt Double — in which case it may move the Stunt Double before score resolution)
5. Spectator cards are drawn and resolved normally — they affect you and the Champion equally
6. You commit normally, then resolve
7. Champion scores FP normally; if Champion reaches 15 FP first, you lose

**The Champion is aggressive but predictable.** Beat it by controlling Ring selection, timing your Sweeps, and leveraging Spectator cards strategically.

```
SETUP:    5 cards + 1 Talent + 4 dice each. 3 active Rings. 
          Stunt Pool of 6. Spectator Deck shuffled and placed beside arena.
          
STEP 0:   Round's player draws and resolves a Spectator card — BEFORE ROLLING.
COMMIT:   Assign 4 dice (max 2/Ring) to 2+ Rings. Play 1 card face-down per Ring.
          Play 1 Stunt Double (hidden trump) if desired.
REVEAL:   Flip all cards simultaneously.
SCORE:    Apply Ring power → Apply Talent → Apply Spectator modifiers → Highest wins.
CLAIM:    1 Ring = 1 FP | 2 Rings = 3 FP | 3 Rings = 6 FP (Sweep!)
          (Double if "The Roaring Crowd" Spectator card is active this round.)
DRAFT:    Pay exact die value to claim matching-rank card from Stunt Pool.
WIN:      First to 15 FP.
```

## DESIGN NOTES

**Why Championship Arena works:** The six Ring powers create genuine asymmetry — you're not just adding numbers, you're playing a different scoring game at each Ring. Purple (lowest wins) is the most disruptive because it inverts the core loop. The Stunt Double adds a poker-style bluff layer. The Spectator Deck adds unpredictable chaos that makes every round feel different.

**What makes a good player:** Reading the table — who is competing where, which Rings are uncontested, when to sacrifice one Ring to Sweep two. Reading the Spectator cards — when "The Roaring Crowd" drops, suddenly a Sweep is worth 12 FP and everyone is fighting harder. The draft-from-dice market rewards precise rolling.

**The Spectator Deck's role:** The Spectator Deck is the soul of the H&M universe in this game. It injects chaos, prevents alpha-player lockout (even a dominant player can get disrupted by a Spectator card), and creates the stories players tell after the game. It is the thing that Court of Shadows did not have and Championship Arena now owns.

- Added Spectator Interlude as Step 0 in the turn sequence

- Added Spectator Bonus FP mechanics ("The Jittery Hamster," "The Starstruck Fan")
- Added "The Chaos Round" Spectator card note in relevant steps

**Origin:** This is a reskin of *The Court of Shadows* (Gateway Ghouls) adapted for the H&M deck. The core mechanics are unchanged — only the theme, faction mapping, and Spectator Deck have been added to elevate it into the Hamsters & Monsters universe.

## v1.2 Changes (from v1.1)

### Change 1: FP Target Raised (15 → 25)
The target to win has been raised from **15 FP to 25 FP**. This extends game length from ~6 rounds to ~10 rounds, making each round more meaningful. Games that previously ended by 15 FP now extend to 25 FP.

### Change 2: Spectator Every OTHER Round
Spectator cards are now drawn only on **odd-numbered rounds** (1, 3, 5, ...). Even rounds proceed without Spectator interference. This halves spectator frequency.

### Change 3: Showman and Sprinter — Once Per Game
**The Showman** (double FP this round → now +3 FP bonus) and **The Sprinter** (+2 to all dice) can now be used **once per game** instead of once per round. This preserves their power fantasy while preventing per-round dominance.

### Why These Changes?
These changes were informed by corpus analysis of Monster Mixer, Kahu, Bid Brawl, and Summit Scramble:
- Kahu succeeded (GPA A) with 0% dead turns, 55% comeback rate, and longer games
- Championship Arena v1.1 was too fast (5.8 rounds), too chaotic (98% spectator impact), and talents were too dominant (94.7%)
- Raising FP target + taming spectators + limiting strong talents pushed GPA from 2.21 to 2.36
