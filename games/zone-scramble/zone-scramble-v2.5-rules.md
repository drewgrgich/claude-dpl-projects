# ZONE SCRAMBLE v2.5
## The Simulator Edition
## March 15, 2026

---

## What Changed from v2.4.3

Six changes based on 4,000+ AI-simulated games. Goals: eliminate draws, create meaningful opening decisions, and level the faction power curve.

1. **Cascading Tiebreaker** — Ties dropped from 10.5% to 0%. Three-layer tiebreaker replaces the old "exact rank 15" rule that almost never fired.
2. **Center Arena Bonus** — Winning the Center arena awards +2 VP instead of +1. Creates real tension from Turn 1: commit to the high-value middle, or cede it and take both flanks for Momentum?
3. **RED Bodyguard Nerf** — Now requires 2+ of your Monsters in the arena before triggering. Prevents a solo Red 10 from scoring as 12. Orange+Red win rate dropped from 63% to 46%.
4. **BLUE Swap Buff** — "Is This Your Card?" range expanded from within 2 ranks to within 3. Blue pairs went from ~30% win rate to ~53%.
5. **YELLOW Fast Start Rework** — Changed from +2 on your first Monster to +1 on each of your first two Monsters per round. Same total value, but now affects two scoring events instead of one.
6. **PURPLE Time Capsule Buff** — Keep 2 cards between rounds instead of 1. Gives Purple a real "planning ahead" identity.

## Overview

**Players:** 2 only
**Time:** 12-15 minutes
**Components:** 66-card deck (ranks 0-10, 6 factions)

**The Pitch:** Two hamster trainers coach their monster squads through three training arenas. First monster claims the turf. Fifth monster makes the crowd roar. Draft your dream team, play your cards, use your powers wisely.

---

## Win Condition

Play **3 rounds**. Score arenas as the Crowd Roars.

**VP:**
- Win a side arena (Left or Right): **+1 VP**
- Win the **Center** arena: **+2 VP**
- Win **2+ arenas** in a round: **+1 VP Momentum**

After 3 rounds, highest VP wins.

**Tiebreaker** (resolve in order, stop as soon as one breaks the tie):
1. **Most arenas won** across the entire game
2. **Highest single-arena rank total** achieved in any scoring
3. **Most recent scorer** — whoever won the last arena to be scored

## Setup (90 seconds)

### 1. Faction Draft (No Sorting)

Shuffle the deck. Reveal cards from top until you've seen **5 different factions**. (Discard revealed cards after.)

Snake draft **4 of those 5** factions:
- Start player picks **1 faction**
- Other player picks **2 factions**
- Start player picks **1 faction**

Each player has **2 Command Factions**.

### 2. Deal & Arenas

- Deal **6 cards** to each player
- Place deck as draw pile
- Create **3 arenas**: **Left / Center / Right**
- Designate space for **Trophy Pile** (Orange stolen cards) and **discard pile**

---

## Core Rules

### The Turf Color Rule

The **first Monster** played into an empty arena claims that arena's **Turf Color**.

While the arena has a Turf Color, you may play there only if:
- Your Monster matches the **Turf Color**, OR
- It's a **Chameleon** (rank **0 or 10**)

When the Crowd Roars (arena scores), it clears and becomes Turf-less.

### Chameleon Monsters (0 and 10)

Chameleons blend in anywhere:
- May be played to any arena
- Count as the **Turf Color** for legality
- In an empty arena, you choose its color (becomes Turf Color)

**Limit:** Max **2 Chameleons per round per player**.

### Fumble the Bag (Chaos Play)

Your Trainer got flustered and **dropped the equipment**:
- **Discard 1 card**
- **Draw 1 card** (if draw pile has cards)
- End turn

**Limit:** Max **2 Fumbles per round** per player.

### The Bench (Forced Pass)

If you **cannot legally play a Monster** (no matching Turf Color in any arena, no Chameleons remaining), and you have **exhausted both Fumbles**:

1. **Reveal your hand** to prove the deadlock
2. **Discard 1 card** to The Stands
3. **Draw 1 card** (if draw pile has cards)
4. End your turn

*This is a last resort — you are bleeding tempo and cards. The game continues.*

### Empty Draw Pile

If the **draw pile is empty**, you don't draw. The discard pile remains as is. Continue play until round ends.

---

## Turn Structure

**3 rounds. 6 turns each per round.**

On your turn:
1. **Play 1 Monster** to a legal arena **OR** **Fumble the Bag** **OR** **Take The Bench** (if locked out)
2. If you played a Monster and draw pile has cards, **draw 1 card**
3. You may use **one Signature Move** (max 1 per turn)

Start player alternates each round.

---

## The Crowd Roars

An arena **immediately scores** when it has **5 Monsters**.

Count to five. The crowd goes wild.

### Scoring an Arena

Add up **total ranks** each player contributed (including personality bonuses).
- Higher total wins: **+1 VP** (or **+2 VP** for Center)
- Ties: nobody scores

Clear the arena to discard pile. It becomes Turf-less.

### End of Round

After both players take 6 turns:
1. Score any arenas that haven't roared yet
2. Award **Momentum** (+1 VP) if a player won **2+ arenas**
3. **Discard all cards remaining in your hand**
4. Each player draws **6 new cards** for next round

*Exception: See Purple's Time Capsule.*

## Monster Powers (v2.5)

### 🔴 RED — Super-Dupes
**Personality: Bodyguard** — When you have **2 or more** of your Monsters in an arena, your highest-rank Monster there gets **+1 rank** for scoring.

*Solo Monsters don't get the bonus — the bodyguard needs someone to protect.*

**Signature Move — Heroic Intervention (1/round):**
After playing Red, move **one of your Monsters** from an **adjacent** arena into this arena (must be legal).

*Adjacent: Left ↔ Center ↔ Right*

---

### 🟠 ORANGE — Finders-Keepers
**Personality: Dibs Mine** — First Orange you play into an arena with no other Orange there is **+1 rank** for scoring.

**Signature Move — THE Big Borrow (ONCE PER GAME):**
When you **lose** an arena by **3 or less**, take **one Monster of rank 1–5** from that arena. Place it **face-up in your Trophy Pile** (worth **+1 VP** at game end).

*Place the Orange card you played **sideways** as a reminder you used this.*

*Trophy Pile cards are out of play. They don't return to deck or hand.*

---

### 🟡 YELLOW — Tinkerers
**Personality: Fast Start** — The first **2 Monsters** you play each round each get **+1 rank** for scoring.

*Your opening moves carry extra weight — choose where to invest them.*

**Signature Move — Double-Install (1/round):**
After playing Yellow, immediately play a **second Monster** to the **same arena** (must be legal). Then draw **1 card**.

**If your first Yellow caused the arena to reach 5 Monsters, the arena roars immediately.** The second Monster is not played; draw normally.

---

### 🟢 GREEN — Prognosticationers
**Personality: I Knew That** — Once per turn before playing, **look at top card of deck**. Put it back or discard it.

**Signature Move — Scheduled Outcome (1/round):**
After playing Green, **draw 2, keep 1, discard 1**.

---

### 🔵 BLUE — Magicians
**Personality: Misdirection** — When you play Blue, you may **return one of your other Monsters** from that arena to your hand (not the Blue you just played).

**Important:** If the arena reached **5 Monsters at any point this turn, it still roars.** You cannot prevent the roar.

**Signature Move — Is This Your Card? (1/round):**
Swap **one of your Monsters** with **one of opponent's Monsters** in the same arena. **Restriction: printed ranks must be within 3** (Chameleons count as 0 or 10).

---

### 🟣 PURPLE — Time Travelers
**Personality: Time Capsule** — At end of round, when discarding hand and drawing 6, you may **keep 2 cards** instead of discarding them.

*Plan ahead. Hold that Chameleon and a power card for next round.*

**Signature Move — Rewind (1/round):**
After resolving any roar/scoring/clearing from your Purple play, you may take the **top card of the discard pile** into your hand **only if its printed rank is 1–6**.

**You cannot take a card you just discarded as part of another effect this same moment.**

## Optional Modes

### Pop Bonus Mode (Recommended for Dopamine)
When you play the **5th Monster** and cause the arena to Crowd Roar, one of **your Monsters** in that arena gets **+2 rank** for this scoring only.

*Reward without hand advantage snowball.*

### High-Dopamine Mode (Alternative)
When you play the **5th Monster** and cause the arena to Crowd Roar, **draw +1 card** immediately.

*More exciting but may snowball.*

### Pop Tax Mode (If Control Too Strong)
When you play the **5th Monster** and cause the arena to Crowd Roar, **do not draw a card** this turn.

*Makes rushing the pop costly.*

## Appendix: Design Notes (For Developers)

### Balance Data (v2.5)
Based on 2,000-game simulation with all v2.5 changes:

| Metric | v2.4.3 | v2.5 |
|--------|--------|------|
| Tie rate | 10.5% | 0.0% |
| First-player spread | 3.7% | 1.1% |
| Avg VP per player | 7.4 | 9.9 |
| Avg stagnation/game | 3.1 | 2.6 |

### Faction Pair Win Rates (v2.5, 2000 games)

| Pair | Win Rate | Status |
|------|----------|--------|
| Green + Orange | 58.9% | Watch — slightly hot |
| Orange + Yellow | 62.4% | Watch — Orange combos run warm |
| Blue + Green | 55.2% | Healthy (was 29.9% in v2.4.3) |
| Blue + Yellow | 52.9% | Healthy (was 35.7% in v2.4.3) |
| Orange + Red | 45.8% | Healthy (was 63.3% in v2.4.3) |
| Blue + Red | 42.2% | Healthy |
| Blue + Purple | 40.4% | Slightly cool |
| Orange + Purple | 39.7% | Slightly cool |
| Purple + Red | 29.1% | Watch — both factions are reactive |

### Key Design Decisions
- **Center arena bonus:** Creates asymmetric arena value without adding rules weight. Players naturally fight over Center, making flank-Momentum a real alternative strategy.
- **Cascading tiebreaker:** Three layers ensure resolution. "Most arenas won" is intuitive, "best arena" rewards going big, "last scorer" rewards late-game clutch plays.
- **RED Bodyguard nerf:** Requiring 2+ Monsters prevents the free-value-on-solo problem. RED now rewards committing to an arena.
- **BLUE swap at 3 ranks:** Opens up meaningful swap options (3↔6, 4↔7) without enabling the old "steal the 10" concern.
- **YELLOW +1/+1 instead of +2:** Spreads the bonus across two plays, making it visible in two scoring events instead of one.
- **PURPLE keep 2:** Gives the faction a planning identity distinct from Green's card filtering. Purple looks ahead; Green looks at the present.

### Known Edge Cases (Handled)
- Empty draw pile: skip draws ✓
- Blue un-pop exploit: cannot prevent roar ✓
- Orange trophy: face-up pile, out of play ✓
- Purple self-take: prohibited ✓
- Deadlock/lockout: The Bench rule ✓
- VP tie after 3 rounds: cascading tiebreaker ✓

## Teaching Script (60 seconds)

"You're Hamster Trainers coaching Monster squads into three arenas. First Monster claims the Turf Color — only that color or Chameleons can enter. Fifth Monster makes the Crowd Roar — highest total rank wins the VP. The Center arena is worth double.

Draft two Monster types. Each gives you a Personality and a Signature Move. Most VP after three rounds wins.

Can't play legally? Fumble the Bag — discard and draw, max twice per round. Completely locked out? Take The Bench — discard and draw, but prove it by showing your hand."
