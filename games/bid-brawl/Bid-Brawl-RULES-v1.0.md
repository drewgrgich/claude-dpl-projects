# Bid Brawl Rules v1.0

- 58-card deck: standard 52 plus 6 wilds.
- Number cards and faces/Aces bid at rank/points 2-14.
- Wilds cannot be bid and score 0.
- Deal 5 cards to each player.
- Reveal prize market each round: 2P=3, 3P=4, 4P=4, 5P=5.
- All players secretly bid one card simultaneously.
- Highest bid wins first prize pick, then next highest, etc. Ties break Spades > Hearts > Diamonds > Clubs.
- In 3-5P, each bid card goes to the corresponding winner's discard pile. Losing bids feed the winner who beat them in ranking order.
- Hearts: if you win with Hearts, draw 1 bonus card after resolution.
- Diamonds: if you lose with Diamonds, peek at the top deck card.
- Clubs: discard pile is face-up/public.
- Lowest-point player once per game may use Underdog Reroll after reveal: set aside their bid, skip ranking, take one remaining prize; their bid goes to global discard.
- Draw back up to 5. If deck cannot fully replenish all hands, that round is the final round.
- In 2P, play 8 rounds, reveal 3 prizes per round, and each bid goes to the opponent's discard.
- When draw pile empties in 3-5P, shuffle global discard back into the deck.
- End game: score prize-card points only.
