# 🐹 SNACK STASH SCRAMBLE (v1.6 - Live Litter Box Edition)

### A High-Speed Action-Rummy Game for 2–4 Players

**System:** Hamsters & Monsters Core Deck (66 cards) | **Time:** 15–20 Min

### ARCHITECT'S NOTE: WHAT IS THIS GAME?

*Snack Stash Scramble* is a fast, mean, tactical rummy game about converting snacks into points before the pantry runs dry.

The game should feel:

- quick to read
- interactive through the discard pile
- slightly nasty without becoming cruel
- tactical rather than deeply calculational

Do not get attached to your hand. Bank often. Read the Litter Box. Ruin someone else's snack pile when the moment is right.

3. **NEW — Litter Box Swap.** Drawing from the Litter Box now lets you cycle one card back, making discard play more tactical.

8. **CLARIFIED — Natural trigger rule.** Only a Natural Card can trigger a Faction Power.
9. **NEW — Big Extension bonus.** Extending your own stash meaningfully can now create a small combo turn.

## ⚙️ BASE PRINCIPLES: THE DECK PHYSICS

The deck has 66 cards across 6 Factions (Colors). Each Faction has cards ranked **0 through 10**.

### THE WILD GAP

- **Ranks 0 and 10 are WILD.** They can substitute for any number or Faction to complete a set.
- **Wilds do not have an innate Faction.**
- **The Anchor Rule:** If you want a set containing a Wild to trigger a Faction Power, the triggered card must be a **Natural Card (Ranks 1–9)** in that set.
- **Wilds can help complete a set, but they can never be the card you point to when you Fire a Faction.**

This means Wilds are flexible support pieces, not full identity cards.

## 🏁 SETUP

1. **Shuffle** the 66-card deck.
2. **Deal starting hands by seat order (Late to the Pantry rule):**

| Players | Seat 1 | Seat 2 | Seat 3 | Seat 4 |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 2 Players | 7 cards | 7 cards | — | — |
| 3 Players | 6 cards | 7 cards | 7 cards | — |
| 4 Players | 6 cards | 7 cards | 7 cards | 8 cards |

3. Place the remaining deck in the center. This is **The Feeder**.
4. Flip the top card of The Feeder face-up next to it. This starts **The Litter Box**.

---

## 🏃 HOW TO PLAY: THE TURN LOOP

On your turn, complete these steps in order:

1. **Draw**
2. **Bank & Fire**
3. **Discard**

---

## 1. DRAW (Restock)

Count your hand size to determine your draw action:

- **The Snack Floor:** If you begin your turn with **2 or fewer cards**, draw exactly **3 cards** from The Feeder.
- **Standard Draw:** If you begin your turn with **3 or more cards**, draw either:
  - the top card of **The Feeder**, or
  - the top card of **The Litter Box**.
- **Desperate Sniffing:** If you **could not bank any set on your previous turn**, draw **2 cards** from The Feeder instead of 1.  
  You may still choose the Litter Box instead if you prefer the visible card.
- **Snack Floor overrides Desperate Sniffing.** If your hand is 2 or fewer, always use Snack Floor.

### 🔄 Pantry Restock

Whenever you draw from the **Litter Box**, immediately flip the top card of **The Feeder** face-up onto the Litter Box to replace it.

### ♻️ Litter Box Swap

If you draw from the **Litter Box**, you may immediately discard **1 different card** from your hand onto the Litter Box.

This is optional.

You cannot discard the same card you just took.

---

## 2. BANK & FIRE (The Action Phase)

*Optional.* You may play valid sets from your hand onto the table in front of you. This is your **Cheek Stash**.

A valid set is either:

- **A Group:** 3 or more cards of the same number
- **A Run:** 3 or more cards of the same Faction in numerical sequence

### THE DOPAMINE TRIGGER (Firing)

Whenever you bank a **new** set of 3+ cards, you may point to **exactly one Natural Card (Ranks 1–9)** in that set and trigger its Faction Power.

- Wilds may be part of the set.
- Wilds may not be chosen as the trigger card.
- Extending a previously banked set normally does **not** trigger a Faction Power.

### BIG EXTENSION BONUS

Once per turn, if you extend **one of your own banked sets** by **2 or more cards at once**, draw **1 card** from The Feeder immediately.

This does **not** trigger a Faction Power.

It is simply a combo bonus for committing hard to one stash.

---

## 3. DISCARD (End Turn)

End your turn by placing **1 card** from your hand face-up onto The Litter Box.

If you played every card from your hand and have zero left, your turn simply ends.

Play passes clockwise.

---

## 💥 THE DISTRACTIONS (Faction Powers)

When you **Fire** a Faction by banking a new set, resolve its effect immediately.

### 🔴 RED (Super-Dupes) — *"I'll Guard It!"*

Choose one of your banked sets.

- Opponents cannot place Poisoned Peanuts on that set for the rest of the game.
- That set immediately gains **+3 VP**.

This makes Red useful even if nobody was planning to mess with you yet.

### 🟠 ORANGE (Finders-Keepers) — *"Dibs!"*

Take the top card of the Litter Box into your hand. Then discard **1 card** from your hand to the Litter Box.

Orange is still efficient, but now asks for hand management instead of giving free value.

### 🟡 YELLOW (Tinkerers) — *"Look What I Did!"*

Immediately play a single valid card from your hand to extend **any** player's banked set on the table.

**Poisoned Peanut:**  
If you place a card on an opponent's set, turn it sideways to mark it as poisoned. At final scoring, sideways cards **subtract their face value** from that set owner's score instead of adding to it.

If you extend your own set, place the card normally.

### 🟢 GREEN (Prognosticationers) — *"Just As Planned."*

Look at the top **4** cards of The Feeder.

- Take **1** into your hand.
- Return the other **3** to the top of The Feeder in any order.

Green is now a real engine instead of a mild informational nudge.

### 🔵 BLUE (Magicians) — *"Is This Your Card?"*

Take a random card from an opponent's hand. Give them **1 card** of your choice from your hand.

**Empty Pockets Contingency:** If all opponents have 0 cards in hand, draw 1 card from The Feeder instead.

### 🟣 PURPLE (Time Travelers) — *"Nap Time."*

Take any 1 card from the Litter Box and slide it to the bottom of The Feeder.

Purple manipulates the game clock and denies specific discard options.

---

## 🚨 THE HALFTIME SWEEP

When **The Feeder** empties for the first time:

1. Leave the top card of the Litter Box where it is.
2. Take the rest of the Litter Box.
3. Shuffle it to form a new Feeder.
4. Resume play immediately.

---

## 💥 ENDGAME: THE GREEDY CHEEKS CHECK

The game ends **instantly** the exact moment The Feeder empties for the **second** time.

No final turn. No cleanup turn. No mercy.

### 🚨 MID-BITE WHISTLE

If the Feeder empties for the second time while a player is in the middle of drawing multiple cards for **The Snack Floor**, the game ends immediately when the final card is pulled.

That player does not continue the turn.

---

## SCORING

### 1. Count Your Stash

Add the face value of every card you banked.

### 2. Wilds Bank for 0

Any banked **0** or **10** is worth **0 VP**.

Wilds help build sets. They do not score like premium snacks.

### 3. Poisoned Peanut Tax

Any sideways cards in your banked sets subtract their face value from your score instead of adding to it.

### 4. RED Bonus

If a set was protected by RED, include its **+3 VP** bonus.

### 5. Greedy Cheeks Penalty

Reveal your hand. Subtract the face value of every card still in your hand from your score.

### 6. Jawbreaker Hazard

Any **0** or **10** left in your hand at game end is worth **-10 VP**.

### 7. Highest Score Wins

The player with the highest final score is the Champion of the Snacks.

---

## QUICK TEACH SUMMARY

- Draw from the Feeder or Litter Box.
- Bank Groups or Runs of 3+.
- New sets trigger one Faction Power from a Natural Card in that set.
- Discard to end turn.
- Litter Box draws replace themselves from the Feeder.
- Wilds help complete sets, but bank for **0** and hurt badly if stranded in hand.
- Yellow can poison opponents' sets.
- Red protects a set and gives **+3 VP**.
- Game ends when the Feeder empties the second time.
