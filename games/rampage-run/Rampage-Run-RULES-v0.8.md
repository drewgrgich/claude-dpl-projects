# RAMPAGE RUN — Game Rules v0.8

*Designer Corps — Phase 2 Draft*
*Iteration 8*

---

## One-Sentence Pitch

Build the shared rampage, bank it before it blows up, and stick the next player with a miserable decision.

---

## Goal

Be the first player to reach **50 or more banked points**.

If no one reaches 50 before the deck runs out, finish the game and the player with the **highest banked score** wins.

---

## Components

- **66 cards**
- **6 monster-crew factions**
- ranks **0–10** in each faction

### Wild 0s

A **0** is wild.
- It may count as **any rank from 1 to 10** when you play it.
- It still adds **0 points** to the Streak Total.
- When you play a 0, you must **declare which rank it counts as**. That declared rank becomes the new **Current Target**.

If the opening center card is a **0**, reveal cards from the deck until you reveal a rank **1–10** card instead. Shuffle the revealed 0 back into the deck.

---

## Setup

1. Shuffle all 66 cards.
2. Deal **5 cards** to each player.
3. Put the rest face-down as the draw pile.
4. Flip 1 card to the center to begin the first Streak.
   - that card’s rank becomes the **Current Target**
   - that card’s value becomes the **Streak Total**
5. The player with the **lowest rank in hand** takes the first turn.
   - Ignore 0s for this check.
   - If tied, tied players compare their next-lowest non-0 card.
   - If still tied, start with the tied player closest clockwise to the dealer.
6. No one is the **Last Driver** yet.

---

## Center Area

Keep three things visible:

- **Current Target** — the rank a played card must meet or beat
- **Streak Total** — the points currently at stake
- **Last Driver** — the most recent player who played a card into the Streak

---

## Turn Structure

On your turn, first check whether you have a legal play.

A legal play is **1 card** from your hand that is:
- equal to the Current Target
- higher than the Current Target
- or a **0 wild**

Then choose **one** of these actions:

### 1) Play
Play 1 legal card from your hand into the center.

After you play:
- add that card’s printed value to the **Streak Total**
- the card’s rank becomes the new **Current Target**
  - if it was a 0, use the rank you declared
- you become the new **Last Driver**

After resolving the play, you must choose one of these:
- **Pass** — end your turn and leave the Streak for the next player
- **Fold** — immediately bank the entire Streak yourself

### 2) Fold
Bank the current Streak without adding a card.

When you Fold:
- you score the full **Streak Total**
- discard all cards in the center Streak
- refill your hand to **5 cards**
- start a new Streak if possible
- end your turn

You may Fold even if you have a legal play.

### 3) Bust
You may Bust **only if you have at least 1 legal play**.

When you Bust:
- discard all cards in the center Streak
- if your helmet is still intact, spend it and **nobody scores**
- if your helmet is already gone, the **Last Driver** scores the full **Streak Total** as a **Crash Bonus**
- refill your hand to **5 cards**
- start a new Streak if possible
- end your turn

### If You Cannot Play
If you have **no legal play**, you cannot choose Play or Bust.

You must:
- **Fold if the Streak Total is greater than 0**
- if there is somehow no active Streak, simply refill only if instructed by another effect (normally this will not happen)

In normal play, “cannot play” means you are forced to bank the current Streak for yourself.

---

## Starting a New Streak

Whenever a Fold or Bust clears the center:

1. The active player refills to **5 cards** from the draw pile.
2. If at least 1 card remains in the draw pile, flip 1 card to begin a new Streak.
3. That card’s rank becomes the new **Current Target** and its value becomes the new **Streak Total**.
4. Set **Last Driver** to **none**.

If the draw pile does not have enough cards, draw as many as possible.

---

## Helmets

Each player has **1 helmet for the whole game**.

The first time you Bust:
- your helmet absorbs the damage
- the Streak is discarded
- **no Crash Bonus is paid**
- your helmet is gone for the rest of the game

Any later Bust by that same player is unprotected.

Use any reminder you want: token, coin, dial, sideways card, etc.

---

## Scoring Summary

| Event | Who scores? | Points |
|---|---|---:|
| Fold without playing | active player | full Streak Total |
| Play, then Fold | active player | full Streak Total |
| First Bust by that player | nobody | 0 |
| Later Bust by that player | Last Driver | full Streak Total |
| Play, then Pass | nobody yet | 0 |

---

## End of Game

### Instant Win
If a player reaches **50 or more banked points**, they win immediately.

### Final Stretch After the Deck Runs Out
Once the draw pile cannot provide cards anymore:
- keep playing the current Streak normally
- players do **not** refill cards they cannot draw
- if a Fold or Bust clears the center and there is no card left to flip, the game enters the **final lap**
- in the final lap, each remaining player, including the player who triggered it, gets **1 more turn**
- after those turns, the game ends

### Busts After the Deck Is Empty
Once no new Streak can be started from the deck:
- Bust still clears the current Streak
- **no Crash Bonus is awarded**

### Ties
If banked scores are tied:
1. player with more cards in hand wins
2. if still tied, player with fewer Busts wins
3. if still tied, each tied player draws 1 card from the discard pile at random; highest rank wins, redraw tied ranks

---

## 2-Player Rule

In a 2-player game, **Bust never awards a Crash Bonus**.

A Bust simply clears the Streak, the active player refills to 5 if possible, and a new Streak begins if possible.

---

## Quick Teach

On your turn:
- **Play** a card that meets or beats the target
- then either **Pass** or **Fold**
- or **Fold** right away
- or **Bust** to blow up the Streak on purpose

Remember:
- **0s are wild, but worth 0 points**
- **when you play a 0, declare its rank**
- **your first Bust spends your helmet**
- **after that, your Bust can pay the Last Driver**
- **first to 50 wins**

---

## Theme Hook

Each faction is a different giant-monster crew tearing through the same district. The Streak is the shared path of destruction: you can extend it, cash it in, or blow it apart before somebody else profits.

---

## Why This Draft

This version is aimed at cleaner teaching and fewer rules questions:
- clarifies what a wild 0 does when played
- removes ambiguity around “cannot play” turns
- defines exactly when **Last Driver** exists or resets
- cleans up endgame timing once the deck runs out
- keeps the core push-your-luck / screw-the-next-player feel intact

---

## Playtest Checklist

- [ ] Do players understand that **Play** now splits into **Pass or Fold**?

- [ ] Are Crash Bonuses creating fun tension instead of kingmaking resentment?
- [ ] Do players remember to declare a rank on wild 0s?
- [ ] Does the final-lap rule end the game cleanly?
