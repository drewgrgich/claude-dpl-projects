# RAMPAGE RUN — Game Rules v0.7

*Designer Corps — Phase 2 Draft*
*Iteration 7 of ~7*

---

## One-Sentence Pitch

Build the shared rampage, bank it before it blows up, and force the next player into a bad choice.

---

## Goal

Reach **50+ banked points** first, or have the most banked points when the deck is empty and the final round ends.

---

## Components

- **66 cards**
- **6 monster-crew factions**
- ranks **0–10** in each faction

**0 is wild:** it may count as any rank **1–10** for matching, but still adds **0 points**.

---

## Setup

1. Shuffle all 66 cards.
2. Deal **5 cards** to each player.
3. Put the rest face-down as the draw pile.
4. Flip 1 card to start the center pile.
   - its rank is the **Current Target**
   - its value starts the **Streak Total**
5. Starting player: whoever has the lowest rank in hand. Ties break clockwise.

---

## What You See in the Center

- **Current Target** = rank you must meet or beat
- **Streak Total** = points currently at stake
- **Last Driver** = the player who most recently added a card

---

## Your 3 Choices

### 1) PLAY
Add 1 legal card from your hand.

A card is legal if it is:
- equal to the Current Target
- higher than the Current Target
- or a **0 wild**

After you play:
- your card becomes the new Current Target
- add its printed value to the Streak Total

### 2) FOLD
Take the points now.

- score the full Streak Total
- discard the center pile
- draw back up to **5 cards**
- if the draw pile still has cards, flip 1 to begin a new Streak
- end your turn

You may Fold even if you could Play.

### 3) BUST
If you **could legally Play**, you may instead wipe the center pile.

- discard the center pile
- if your **helmet** is already gone, the **Last Driver** scores the full Streak Total as a **Crash Bonus**
- if your helmet is still intact, spend it and nobody scores the bonus
- draw back up to **5 cards**
- if the draw pile still has cards, flip 1 to begin a new Streak
- end your turn

**If you cannot legally Play, you do not Bust. You simply Fold for 0.**

---

## Post-Play Fold

After you Play, you may immediately **Fold** and bank the whole Streak yourself.

### Example
- Current Target is **6**
- Streak Total is **14**
- you play an **8**, raising the total to **22**
- you instantly Fold and score **22**

---

## Helmet Rule

Each player has **1 helmet** for the whole game.

The first time you Bust:
- the center pile is lost
- **no bonus** is paid
- your helmet is gone for the rest of the game

Use any reminder you want: coin, token, or sideways card.

---

## Scoring Table

| Event | Who scores? | Points |
|---|---|---:|
| Fold | active player | full Streak Total |
| First Bust | nobody | 0 |
| Later Bust | Last Driver | full Streak Total |
| Play and pass | nobody yet | 0 |

---

## End of Game

### Instant Win
A player who reaches **50+** banked points wins immediately.

### Empty Deck
When the draw pile runs out:
- finish the current round so everyone gets one last turn
- if a reset would start a new Streak but there is no card to flip, the current Streak keeps going if one exists
- once the deck is empty, a Bust clears the Streak but awards **no bonus**
- highest banked score wins

### Ties
1. most cards in hand
2. fewest Busts
3. sudden-death extra round

---

## 2-Player Rule

In a 2-player game, Bust never awards that bonus.

The pile is cleared, hands refill, and play continues.

---

## Quick Teach

On your turn:
- **Play** to keep the run alive
- **Fold** to bank it
- **Bust** to blow it up on purpose

Also remember:
- **0s are wild**
- **first Bust is protected by your helmet**
- **first to 50 wins**

---

## Theme Hook

Each faction is a different giant-monster crew tearing through the same district. The shared Streak is the trail of destruction everyone is extending, cashing in, or detonating during the rampage.

## Why This Draft

- shorter teach
- clearer action names
- cleaner first-read structure
- stronger monster-city framing without adding extra rules
