**[KAHU]{.smallcaps}**

[RULEBOOK]{.smallcaps}

*2--4 Players · Ages 12+ · 30--45 Minutes*

*The volcano stirs. Sacred offerings must be gathered before the lava
consumes the village. Build your deck, harvest Pua, and complete your
offerings --- whoever earns the most honour when the danger passes is
crowned Kahu.*

# **[GAME SETUP]{.smallcaps}**

Follow these steps in order to prepare the game. All steps apply to
every player count (2--4 players) unless otherwise noted.

## **1. Prepare the Board**

1.  Place the board in the centre of the table.

2.  Place the Lava Flow Tracker Marker on the Lava Track.\
    (22 for 4 players / 20 for 3 players / 16 for 2 players)

3.  Shuffle the Offering deck (8 cards). Deal 4 face-up into the
    Offering display area on the board. Return the remaining 4 to the
    box without looking at them.

4.  Place a set of VP tokens (4, 3, 2, and 1) face-up on each of the 4
    active Offering cards. These tokens represent the prize for
    completing each Offering --- the first player to complete it earns
    the most.

5.  Separate the 4 Tiki cards from the Market Deck and place them
    face-up in the Tiki display area on the board. They are not shuffled
    into the market.

6.  Shuffle the Market Deck. Deal 5 cards face-up into the 5 market row
    spaces. The rightmost space grants a 1 Influence discount.

## **2. Prepare the Pua Market**

7.  Place all 33 Pua tokens (11 Red, 11 Blue, 11 Yellow) in a shared
    supply accessible to all players.

8.  Randomly assign each of the three Pua colours a starting price of 3,
    4, or 5 Influence --- one price per colour, no two colours share the
    same price.

> *★ Record the starting prices somewhere visible. The Pua market will
> shift throughout the game as tokens are purchased.*

## **3. Prepare the Surf Cards**

9.  Place all 10 Surf cards in a face-up stack beside the board as a
    shared supply. These are always available to purchase for 2
    Influence. They are never shuffled into the Market Deck.

## **4. Player Setup**

Each player performs the following steps simultaneously:

10. Take a starting deck of 11 cards.

11. Find the Lava Flow card in your starting deck and place it directly
    into your discard pile face-up. Do not shuffle it into your draw
    pile.

12. Shuffle your remaining 10 cards to form your draw pile.

13. Draw 5 cards from the top of your draw pile to form your opening
    hand.

**Each player\'s starting deck contains:**

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Card**                                          **Qty**
  ------------------------------------------------- ---------------------
  **Lava Flow**                                     1

  **Hula**                                          1

  **1 Influence**                                   6

  **1 Influence + Red Pua icon**                    1

  **1 Influence + Blue Pua icon**                   1

  **1 Influence + Yellow Pua icon**                 1
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

> *★ The Lava Flow card is placed face-up in your discard pile at the
> start, not your draw pile. It will cycle into your hand naturally on
> an early turn.*

## **5. Starting Player**

The last player to swim in the ocean goes first. Play proceeds
clockwise.

# **[YOUR TURN]{.smallcaps}**

On your turn, follow these four steps in order. You may never skip or
rearrange them.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Step**    **Action**
  ----------- -----------------------------------------------------------
  **1**       Play Cards

  **2**       Spend Influence

  **3**       Refresh the Market

  **4**       Cleanup & Draw
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

## **Step 1 --- Play Cards**

Your entire hand is played as a single unit. Reveal all cards face-up at
once. You then activate card abilities and count your Influence in
whatever order works best for you. You do not play cards one at a time.

However, one rule always applies first:

### **Resolving the Lava Flow Card**

If the Lava Flow card is in your hand at the start of your turn, you
MUST resolve it immediately --- before activating any other card
abilities or counting any Influence. This is always the very first thing
that happens on your turn.

**If you have a Tiki in play:** you MUST discard the Tiki to your
discard pile. The Lava Tracker does not advance. The Tiki has shielded
you from the flow.

**If you have no Tiki in play:** move the Lava Tracker down 1 space.
Immediately resolve any Escalation Event printed on that space (see Lava
Track section).

> *★ Tiki use is mandatory. You cannot choose to advance lava instead of
> using your Tiki.*

### **Drawing the Lava Flow Mid-Turn**

If a card effect causes you to draw additional cards and the Lava Flow
is among them, it fires immediately at that moment --- even in the
middle of resolving other effects.

The same rule applies: discard your Tiki to shield yourself, or advance
the Lava Tracker.

### **Exception --- Pineapple, Outrigger, and Chicken**

These three cards are the only effects that can send a Lava Flow card to
the discard pile without triggering it:

- **Pineapple ---** draw 2 cards; you may keep or discard any cards
  drawn. If the Lava Flow is drawn, you may choose to discard it. Lava
  does not advance.

- **Outrigger ---** draw 3 cards, discard 1. If the Lava Flow is among
  the drawn cards, you may choose it as your one discard. Lava does not
  advance.

- **Chicken ---** reveal and discard the top card of any player\'s deck;
  you may play it as if it were your own. If the revealed card is a Lava
  Flow, you may choose not to play it. It goes to the discard pile. Lava
  does not advance.

> *★ All other draw effects that pull the Lava Flow into your hand
> trigger it immediately. The Lava Flow card cannot be voluntarily
> discarded by any other means.*

### **Activating Card Abilities**

After dealing with any Lava Flow, activate the abilities on your cards
in whatever order benefits you most. Influence from all cards is counted
together. You are never locked into a specific sequence for non-Lava
effects.

## **Step 2 --- Spend Influence**

Total the Influence generated by all cards in your play area. You may
spend it in any combination and any order on the following actions.
Unspent Influence is lost at the end of your turn.

### **Purchase Market Cards**

Pay the listed Influence cost of any card in the market row. The
rightmost card always costs 1 Influence less (minimum 0). Purchased
cards go directly to your discard pile, not your hand. You may buy any
number of cards per turn.

> *★ Tiki cards are not in the market row. See Claim a Tiki below.*

### **Purchase Surf Cards**

Surf cards are always available for 2 Influence each, as a separate
supply of 10 cards. Purchased Surf cards go to your discard pile. Once
the supply is exhausted, it is gone for the rest of the game.

### **Purchase Pua**

Pua tokens (Red, Blue, Yellow) are the key resource needed to complete
Offerings. To purchase one Pua, you must pay both:

- The current Influence cost of that Pua colour from the Pua market, AND

- Have at least one matching Pua icon in your play area (a Wild /
  rainbow icon counts as any colour).

Pua are purchased one at a time. After each purchase, the Pua market
prices shift:

- If you bought from the 3 or 4 Influence price: that colour rises by 1,
  and the colour already at that new price drops by 1.

- If you bought from the 5 Influence price (or 6 after the Pua Price
  Increase escalation): prices do not change.

> *★ Buying one colour makes it more expensive and lowers another. Watch
> the market carefully.*

### **Complete an Offering**

If you have all the required Pua tokens shown on an Offering card, you
may complete it during this step. Spend the required Pua (return them to
the supply) and immediately take the highest remaining VP token from
that Offering card.

Each Offering may only be completed once per player. Multiple players
can complete the same Offering --- the first earns 4 VP, the second
earns 3, and so on. Offering scoring bonuses are not applied now; they
are resolved during Final Scoring.

If this is your third VP token collected, **the endgame is triggered
immediately** (see Endgame).

### **Claim a Tiki**

Spend exactly 1 Red + 1 Blue + 1 Yellow Pua to claim any available Tiki
card from the board display. The Tiki goes directly into your play area
--- not to your discard pile. It stays in front of you until it absorbs
a Lava Flow.

- You may only have one Tiki in play at a time.

- Tikis have no ongoing abilities. They serve only as Lava Shields.

- Once the Lava Tracker reaches space 12 (Tiki Lockout), no more Tikis
  may be claimed for the rest of the game.

## **Step 3 --- Refresh the Market**

Slide all remaining cards in the market row one space to the right. The
card now in the rightmost space gets the 1 Influence discount. Draw from
the top of the Market Deck to refill all empty spaces.

> *★ After the Lava Track reaches space 10, the rightmost market slot is
> permanently removed. Only 4 cards fill the market from that point on.
> The rightmost of those 4 still receives the discount.*

## **Step 4 --- Cleanup & Draw**

Discard your entire play area at once --- all played cards go to your
discard pile in a single sweep.

**Exception:** Tiki cards remain in your play area. They are never
discarded during cleanup. They stay in front of you until they absorb a
Lava Flow.

Draw 5 new cards from the top of your draw pile. Do not reveal these
cards to other players. If your draw pile runs out, shuffle your entire
discard pile into a new draw pile and continue drawing.

Your turn is now over. Pass to the left.

# **[THE LAVA TRACK]{.smallcaps}**

The Lava Tracker starts on space 20 and moves down each time a player's
Lava Flow card triggers without a Tiki shield. Certain spaces are
threshold spaces --- when the tracker lands on or passes one, its
Escalation Event fires immediately, before the current player continues
their turn.

  -------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Space**   **Escalation Event**
  ----------- -------------------------------------------------------------
  **15**      Second Lava Card --- Each player immediately places a second
              Lava Flow card into their discard pile. From here on, lava
              advances much more frequently.

  **12**      Tiki Lockout --- No Tiki cards may be claimed from the
              display for the rest of the game. Any Tikis already in play
              remain.

  **10**      Market Wipe + Market Shrink --- Immediately discard all cards
              in the market row and refill. The rightmost market slot is
              permanently removed; only 4 cards fill the market from now
              on.

  **7**       Pua Price Increase --- All Pua prices permanently rise by 1.
              The range shifts from 3/4/5 to 4/5/6. The price-shift
              mechanic continues operating within the new range.

  **5**       Market Wipe --- Immediately discard all cards in the market
              row and refill. (The market now holds 4 cards following the
              shrink at space 10.)
  -------------------------------------------------------------------------

> *★ Second Lava cards added at space 15 follow the same rules as the
> original: they cannot be permanently removed from the game, and the
> only way to route them to the discard pile without triggering them is
> Pineapple, Outrigger, or Chicken.*

# **[OFFERINGS]{.smallcaps}**

At the start of the game, 4 of the 8 Offering cards are randomly
selected and placed face-up. A set of VP tokens (4, 3, 2, and 1) is
placed on each active Offering. Each Offering shows a Pua cost and an
endgame scoring bonus.

Completing an Offering is free --- simply spend the required Pua when it
is your turn to spend Influence, then take the highest remaining VP
token from that card. The first player to complete a given Offering
earns 4 VP from it; the second earns 3; the third earns 2; the fourth
earns 1.

Pua costs use a letter code: R = Red, B = Blue, Y = Yellow. Each letter
= 1 Pua of that colour. Example: RRBY = 2 Red + 1 Blue + 1 Yellow.

Each Offering may only be completed once per player. All Offering
bonuses are applied during Final Scoring, not when completed.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Offering Bonus (scored at end of game)**             **Pua Cost**
  ------------------------------------------------------ ----------------
  **1 VP for each card you removed from your deck during RRBY
  the game**                                             

  **1 VP for each different card type in your deck       RBY
  (Flower, Item, Wildlife, Islander, Surf, Tiki --- max  
  6 VP)**                                                

  **1 VP for each Islander card you own at end of game** RBYY

  **1 VP for each Item card you own at end of game**     RBYY

  **1 VP for each Wildlife card you own at end of game** RBBY

  **1 VP for each Flower card you own at end of game**   RBY

  **3 VP for each Tiki card you own at end of game**     RBY

  **1 VP for each Surf card you own at end of game**     RBY
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

# **[ENDGAME]{.smallcaps}**

The game ends in one of two ways:

- A player collects their third VP token (completes their third
  Offering). The village is saved --- this is the intended win
  condition.

- The Lava Tracker reaches space 0. The volcano erupts --- play still
  continues to final scoring; nothing is lost immediately.

## **Completing the Round**

As soon as either condition is met, finish the current round so that all
players have taken the same number of turns. This eliminates the
advantage of going first.

> *★ If the Lava Tracker reaches 0 mid-round, continue to the end of the
> round normally before scoring.*

## **Final Scoring**

After the round is complete, all players score simultaneously. Scores
are tallied in three parts:

### **1. Card VP**

Count the printed VP value on every card in your draw pile, hand, and
discard pile. Include any Tiki still in your play area. Cards that were
permanently removed from the game do not score.

### **2. Offering Bonuses**

For each Offering you completed (one for which you hold a VP token),
apply its bonus now. Count the relevant cards or resources in your
current deck and score accordingly.

Example: if you completed "1 VP per Wildlife card you own" and have 6
Wildlife cards across your deck and discard pile, you score 6 bonus VP
from that Offering.

### **3. VP Tokens**

Add the face value of all VP tokens you collected from Offerings. You
may have collected up to three tokens over the course of the game, each
worth between 1 and 4 VP.

## **Declaring the Winner**

Add all three scoring categories together: Card VP + Offering Bonuses +
VP Tokens. The player with the highest total is crowned Kahu.

## **Tiebreaker**

If two or more players are tied, the player with the most unspent Pua
tokens wins. If still tied, all tied players share the victory.

*Aloha and good luck. May the lava spare your village.*
