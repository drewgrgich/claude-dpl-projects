# [Working Title] — Rules v0.1.4

*2–5 players | Ages 10+ | 15–20 minutes*

---

## Overview

Compete to control four colored zones over four rounds. Each round, all players simultaneously place cards from their hand face-down at zones on their player board, then reveal at once. The strongest presence at each zone wins Victory Points. The player with the most VP after four rounds wins.

---

## Components

- **44 Number Cards** — 4 colors (Red, Yellow, Green, Blue) × 11 cards each
  - Each color has: two 0s, two 1s, one 3, one 5, one 7, two 9s, two 10s
- **4 Mascots** — one per color (special: doubles your best card)
- **4 Action Cards** — one per color, each with a unique effect:
  - **Red — Shield** (resolution order: 1st)
  - **Yellow — Bomb** (resolution order: 2nd)
  - **Green — Swap** (resolution order: 3rd)
  - **Blue — Bounty** (resolution order: 4th)
- **4 Duds** — one per color (look like action cards; play as rank 5)
- **12 Condition Cards** — modify the rules for one round
- **4 Zone Cards** — placed in the center of the table, one per color
- **5 Player Boards** — fold-up flap boards with 4 zone slots each
- **Score Pad**

**Total deck: 56 cards** (44 number + 4 mascot + 4 action + 4 dud)

---

## Setup

1. **Place the 4 Zone Cards** face-up in a row in the center of the table: Red, Yellow, Green, Blue.
2. **Shuffle the 56-card deck** and deal each player a hand:

| Players | Cards Dealt | Cards Hidden |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| 2       | 24          | 8           |
| 3       | 16          | 8           |
| 4       | 12          | 8           |
| 5       | 9           | 11          |

3. **Pass cards.** Each player selects cards from their hand and passes them face-down to the player on their left. Pick up the cards passed to you.

| Players | Cards Passed |
|---------|-------------|
| 2       | 4           |
| 3       | 3           |
| 4       | 2           |
| 5       | 2           |

4. **Shuffle the 12 Condition Cards** face-down in a pile near the zones.
5. Each player takes a **Player Board** and places it in front of them.

---

## Round Structure

The game is played over **4 rounds**. Each round follows four phases:

### Phase 1 — Condition

Flip the top **Condition Card** and read it aloud. This condition applies to all players for the entire round. *(Round 1 variant: for your first game, skip the Condition Card in Round 1 so new players learn the base rules first.)*

### Phase 2 — Deploy

All players simultaneously choose cards from their hand and place them **face-down** in the zone slots on their player board. You may place any number of cards at any zone, subject to any restrictions from the active Condition Card.

**Minimum play:** You must play at least **3 cards per round** (2 at 5 players), or all remaining cards if you have fewer. You are not required to play *all* your cards — any cards you keep will be available in future rounds.

Once all players are ready, say "Reveal."

### Phase 3 — Reveal & Resolve

All players flip their cards face-up at the same time.

**Resolve Action Cards** at each zone in this fixed order:

1. **Shield** — This player's stack at this zone is protected. No other action cards can affect their cards here. If this player loses the zone, they still score 2 VP.
2. **Bomb** — The single highest-ranked number card at this zone (across all unshielded players) is discarded before scoring. If tied, the Bomb player chooses which to discard. *(The Bomb can hit the Bomb player's own cards.)*
3. **Swap** — This player may exchange one number card from this zone with one of their own number cards at **any other zone they occupy**. Choose the swap that most improves this zone's strength.
4. **Bounty** — If this player wins this zone, they score **double VP** for it. If they do not win (including ties), they score **0 VP** at this zone (overrides Shield).

**Duds** have no special effect when revealed. They simply count as a **rank 5 card** of their color.

### Phase 4 — Score

Calculate **Strength** at each zone for every player who placed cards there:

> **Strength = Best Card Rank + 2 per Extra Card + 3 Home Field Bonus**

- **Best Card Rank:** The highest rank among your number cards (and duds) at that zone.
- **Extra Card Bonus:** +2 for each additional card beyond the first. *(The Mascot does not count as an extra card — see below.)*
- **Home Field Bonus:** +3 if at least one **natural card** in your stack matches the zone's color. Mascots and Action Cards cannot anchor Home Field. A Dud of the matching color *does* anchor Home Field (it counts as a natural rank 5).

**Mascot:** If a Mascot is in your stack, it **doubles** the rank of your best card. The Mascot's contribution is the doubling — it does not also add +2 as an extra card.

**Winning a zone:** The player with the highest Strength wins that zone and scores **5 VP** (modified by any active Condition or Bounty). If tied, the tied players split the VP, **rounded down**. If no one played at a zone, no one scores.

**2nd Place:** At each zone with a clear winner (no tie for 1st), the player with the second-highest Strength scores **1 VP**. If multiple players tie for 2nd place, they each receive 1 VP. There is no 2nd-place award when the zone is tied for 1st or uncontested.

Record scores on the score pad.

## Strength Examples

**Simple play:** You play a 7 and a 3 at the Blue zone.
> Best card: 7. Extra cards: 1 (+2). Home Field: neither card is Blue, so +0.
> **Strength = 7 + 2 = 9**

**Home Field:** You play a Red 10 and a Red 3 at the Red zone.
> Best card: 10. Extra cards: 1 (+2). Home Field: Red 10 is a natural matching Red zone (+3).
> **Strength = 10 + 2 + 3 = 15**

**Mascot combo:** You play a Green Mascot and a Green 9 at the Green zone.
> Best card: 9, doubled by Mascot = 18. Extra cards: 0 (Mascot doesn't count). Home Field: Green 9 is a natural matching Green zone (+3).
> **Strength = 18 + 3 = 21**

**Mascot without Home Field:** You play a Red Mascot and a Blue 10 at the Yellow zone.
> Best card: 10, doubled by Mascot = 20. Extra cards: 0. Home Field: no natural matches Yellow (+0).
> **Strength = 20**

**Dud at matching zone:** You play a Yellow Dud at the Yellow zone.
> Best card: 5 (dud counts as rank 5). Home Field: Yellow Dud counts as a natural Yellow card (+3).
> **Strength = 5 + 3 = 8**

**Low card play:** You play two 0s at the Red zone.
> Best card: 0. Extra cards: 1 (+2). Home Field: depends on colors.
> **Strength = 0 + 2 = 2** (minimum — useful as zone padding or under Inversion)

---

## Game End

After **4 rounds**, the player with the most VP wins.

**Tiebreaker:** If tied, the player who won more total zones wins. If still tied, the players share the victory.

---

## Condition Cards

One Condition Card is flipped at the start of each round. It applies to all players equally.

### Card Restrictions

| Condition | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| **Naturals Only** | You may not play Mascots this round. (Action cards and duds are still allowed.) |
| **Rainbow** | At each zone, no two of your cards may share a color. (You can play same-color cards, just at different zones.) |
| **Light Touch** | You may play at most 4 cards total this round. |

### Placement Rules

| Condition | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| **All In** | All cards you play this round must go to at most 2 zones. |
| **Spread Out** | You must play cards at 2 or more different zones this round. |

### Scoring Twists

| Condition | Effect |
|-----------|--------|
| **Inversion** | Lowest Strength wins each zone this round. |
| **Double Stakes** | Each zone is worth **10 VP** instead of 5 this round. |
| **Neutral Ground** | The Home Field Bonus (+3) does not apply this round. |
| **Sudden Death** | If two or more players tie at a zone, **nobody scores** that zone. |
| **Efficiency** | Ties are broken by the player who played **fewer cards** at that zone (instead of splitting). |
| **Lone Wolf** | +3 VP bonus for each zone where you are the **only player** who placed cards. |
| **Fortify** | +2 VP bonus for each zone where you play **3 or more cards**. |

## Card Anatomy

### Number Cards (44 total)
Each has a **color** (Red, Yellow, Green, Blue) and a **rank** (0–10). The distribution is not uniform — 0s, 1s, 9s, and 10s appear twice per color, while 3, 5, and 7 appear once. This creates tension: high and low cards are common, but mid-range cards are scarce.

### Mascots (4 total)
Each has a **color** but **no rank**. Identifiable by a distinct Mascot symbol. One per color. Doubles the rank of the best card in the same stack.

### Action Cards (4 total)
Each has a **color** and a **unique action type**. Identifiable by an action card border/backing. They do not have a rank, do not contribute to Strength, and do not count as an extra card for the +2 bonus. They activate their effect during the Resolve phase.

### Duds (4 total)
Each has a **color** and shares the same border/backing as Action Cards (opponents cannot tell them apart until revealed). Duds play as a **rank 5 card** of their color. They contribute to Strength normally.

## Quick Reference (printed on Player Board)

**Strength** = Best Rank + 2 per extra card + 3 if natural matches zone

**Mascot** = Doubles best rank (doesn't add +2; doesn't anchor Home Field)

**Home Field** = Need a natural card matching the zone color

**Action Resolution** = 1. Shield → 2. Bomb → 3. Swap → 4. Bounty

**VP per zone** = 5 (winner) / 1 (2nd place) — modified by Conditions

**Ties** = Split VP rounded down / No 2nd-place award on tied zones

**Hand sizes:** 2P=24 | 3P=16 | 4P=12 | 5P=9

**Min play/round:** 3 cards (2 at 5P)

**Pass:** 2P=4 | 3P=3 | 4P=2 | 5P=2

**Ranks per color:** 0, 0, 1, 1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 9, 10, 10 (+ Mascot, Action, Dud)

## Strategy Tips

- **The draft is everything.** Playing cards at their matching zone is almost always correct. The +3 Home Field bonus is decisive — a Red 7 at the Red zone (Strength 10) beats a wild 9 (Strength 9). Use the pass phase to build a hand of colors you want to fight with, and pass away colors you don't need.
- **Pace yourself.** You have 4 rounds but a fixed hand. Dumping everything early leaves you empty later.
- **0s have their uses.** Under Inversion, low cards win. As zone padding, a 0 adds +2 to your best card. In the draft, they're perfect to pass away.
- **10s and 9s are gold.** With two copies of each per color, multiple players may hold power cards. The double 9s and 10s make contested zones more dramatic.
- **Fight for 2nd.** Even losing a zone can earn you 1 VP if you're the runner-up. A single card tossed at a zone you won't win can still pay off over four rounds.
- **Read the Condition.** "Lone Wolf" rewards going where others won't. "Sudden Death" punishes going where everyone else goes. Inversion flips the entire game — save your low cards for when it hits. Adapt each round.
- **Action cards are scarce.** You might get one in your hand, or none. Don't plan around them — plan around your numbers, and use the action card as an opportunistic bonus.
- **Watch the duds.** When an opponent plays a card with the action border, it might be a Bomb — or it might be a 5. The uncertainty is part of the game.
- **Pass strategically.** You choose cards to give your left neighbor. Don't hand them the card that beats you — but 0s are perfect gifts.

## Designer Notes

This is **v0.1.4** — a working draft for playtesting. The game is currently themed "Tailgate Turf War" (sports/tailgate zone control). Art and a finalized title will come after the mechanics are validated.

### Change Log

- **v0.1.4** — Added 2nd-place VP (1 VP for runner-up at each zone). Scaled minimum cards per round (3 at 2-4P, 2 at 5P). Investigated sniper dominance at 3P — confirmed self-correcting via all-sniper test (33.3% each). Rejected presence VP (score bloat) and 3-zone-at-3P (unnecessary).
- **v0.1.3** — Swap redesigned: targets any zone you occupy (was adjacent only; 0% success → 22-26%). Tie VP rounding changed from ceiling to floor. Added minimum cards per round to prevent hoarding (hoarder win rate at 4P: 39% → 25%). AI overhaul: deployment priority reorder, anchor validation, Bounty strength threshold.
- **v0.1.2** — Reworked 3 condition cards for Bookends compatibility. "No Matching Colors" → "Rainbow" (per-zone color restriction instead of hand-wide). "Max 3 Cards" → "Light Touch" (max 4, scales better). "Spread Out" now requires 2+ zones instead of 3+ (playable at 5P).
- **v0.1.1** — Updated to 56-card deck with Bookends distribution (0,0,1,1,3,5,7,9,9,10,10 per color). Increased to 4 rounds. Adjusted hand sizes and pass counts. Updated rank range from 1–9 to 0–10.
- **v0.1** — Initial design. 48-card custom deck (4 colors × 9 ranks + mascots + actions + duds), 12 Condition Cards, simultaneous deployment, 3 rounds, flat 5 VP/zone.
