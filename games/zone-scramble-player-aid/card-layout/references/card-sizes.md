# Standard Card Sizes

All dimensions at 300 DPI. Bleed adds 1/8" (0.125") per side for professional printing.

## Size Table

| Name | Inches (W × H) | Pixels (W × H) | With Bleed (px) | Common Uses |
|------|----------------|-----------------|------------------|-------------|
| **Poker / Standard** | 2.5 × 3.5 | 750 × 1050 | 825 × 1125 | MTG, Pokemon, most card games |
| **Bridge** | 2.25 × 3.5 | 675 × 1050 | 750 × 1125 | Traditional playing cards |
| **Tarot** | 2.75 × 4.75 | 825 × 1425 | 900 × 1500 | Dixit, Mysterium, tarot decks |
| **Mini Euro** | 1.75 × 2.5 | 525 × 750 | 600 × 825 | Ticket to Ride, small format |
| **Mini American** | 1.625 × 2.5 | 488 × 750 | 563 × 825 | 7 Wonders Duel resources |
| **Square (Small)** | 2.5 × 2.5 | 750 × 750 | 825 × 825 | Tile-style cards |
| **Square (Large)** | 3.5 × 3.5 | 1050 × 1050 | 1125 × 1125 | Large tile cards |
| **Jumbo** | 3.5 × 5.5 | 1050 × 1650 | 1125 × 1725 | Oversized reference cards |
| **Half Letter** | 4.25 × 5.5 | 1275 × 1650 | 1350 × 1725 | Player boards, large refs |
| **Custom** | user-defined | W×300, H×300 | +(75px each side) | Anything non-standard |

## Safe Zones and Margins

For each card, measure inward from the trim edge:

| Zone | Distance from trim | Purpose |
|------|-------------------|---------|
| **Bleed** | 0.125" outside trim | Gets cut off — extend backgrounds here |
| **Trim** | The actual card edge | Where the cut happens |
| **Safe margin** | 0.125" inside trim | No critical content here — cutting is imprecise |
| **Content area** | 0.25"+ inside trim | All text and icons belong here |

For prototyping / home printing (no bleed), the safe margin is the card edge + 0.125" inward.

## Minimum Font Sizes at Print

| Element | Min pt | Min px (300 DPI) |
|---------|--------|-------------------|
| Card title | 10pt | 42px |
| Type/category line | 8pt | 33px |
| Body / ability text | 7pt | 29px |
| Flavor text | 6.5pt | 27px |
| Stats / numbers | 9pt | 37px |
| Fine print | 6pt | 25px |

Body text below 7pt is difficult to read without good lighting. Prefer 8pt (33px) or larger.
