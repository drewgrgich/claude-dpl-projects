# 🏛️ HAMSTER HIGH COUNCIL — MASTER RULES



**System:** Hamsters & Monsters Core Deck **Version:** 2.3 (Final Stable) **Genre:** Dynamic Hierarchy Trick-Taking **Players:** 3–5 | **Time:** 30–45 Min **Premise:** Rival Hamster Factions gather at the High Council. The rules of the house change every round depending on who is sitting in the big chairs.

> **💡 PLAYER HEURISTIC** Winning tricks isn't enough. You must control **who** leads to force the table into a **Council Balance** that favors your hand, while triggering **Faction Talents** to fix your mistakes.

------

## 1. 🎯 THE OBJECTIVE



Score points by winning **Tricks** and adding them to your **Collected Stash**. The first player to reach **50 Victory Points** wins.

- **The Twist:** There are three seats on the High Council. The Factions sitting in those seats dictate the physics of the round.

------

## 2. 📦 SETUP & DEAL



1. **The Snack Vault:** Shuffle the 66-card deck. Place it face-down.
2. **The Dealer:** Randomly determine the first Dealer. Deal passes left each round.
3. **The Deal:** Deal cards based on player count:
   - **3 Players:** Deal **9 Cards**.
   - **4 Players:** Deal **8 Cards**.
   - **5 Players:** Deal **7 Cards**.
4. **Discard Crate:** Face-up discard pile.
5. **Collected Stash:** Each player designates a face-down pile for their won tricks (1 Card = 1 VP).

------

## 3. 🗳️ PHASE 1: COUNCIL BALANCE (Standard)



Reveal cards from the top of the **Snack Vault** one by one until **three different Factions** appear. Discard duplicates to the Crate.

### 🥇 SEAT 1: THE BIG CHEESE (Trump)



- **Assignment:** The **1st unique Faction** revealed.
- **Effect:** This Faction is **TRUMP**. It beats all other suits, regardless of rank.

### 🥈 SEAT 2: THE FAVORITES (Standard)



- **Assignment:** The **2nd unique Faction** revealed.
- **Effect:** This Faction is **ELITE**. It wins ties and follows standard high-rank rules.

### 🥉 SEAT 3: THE WOBBLY SEAT (Chaos)



- **Assignment:** The **3rd unique Faction** revealed.
- **Effect:** This Faction is **UNSTABLE**.
- **The Inversion Rule:** When this Faction is **LED**, the **LOWEST RANK WINS** the trick.
- **⚠️ CRITICAL:** This inversion applies **ONLY** when the Wobbly Faction is led. If a Wobbly card is played as a follow (because you are out of the led suit), it behaves as a standard low rank.

------

## 4. ⚔️ PHASE 2: THE TRICKS



### A. WHO LEADS?



- **Round 1:** Player to the **Left of the Dealer** leads.
- **Subsequent Leads:** The winner of the previous trick leads the next.

### B. PLAYING A CARD



1. **Lead:** Play any card.
2. **Follow:** Moving clockwise, other players **MUST follow suit** if they have it.
3. **Renege:** If you cannot follow suit, you may play **ANY** card.

### C. DETERMINING THE WINNER



Evaluate the cards in this specific order:

1. **THE BIG CHEESE CHECK (Trump)**
   - Are there any cards of the **Seat 1 Faction** played?
   - **YES:** The **Highest Rank** of the Seat 1 Faction wins immediately.
   - **NO:** Proceed to Step 2.
2. **THE WOBBLY CHECK (Inversion)**
   - Was the **Seat 3 Faction (Wobbly)** LED?
   - **YES:** The **Lowest Rank** of the Wobbly Faction wins.
   - **NO:** Proceed to Step 3.
3. **THE STANDARD CHECK**
   - The **Highest Rank** of the **Led Suit** wins.

### D. CLEANUP



The Winner:

1. Collects the trick into their **Collected Stash**.
2. **Triggers a Faction Talent** (See Section 6).
3. Leads the next trick.

------

## 5. 🧪 THE LAWS OF PHYSICS



### 🐣 THE INTERN (Rank 0)



- **Effect:** Whenever you play a **Rank 0** card (regardless of winning or losing), you immediately **DRAW 1 CARD** from the Snack Vault.
- *Note:* Interns are weak and usually lose the trick, but they bring fresh supplies.

### ⏰ COUNCIL RECESS (Ending the Round)



The round ends when **one player runs out of cards**.

1. **Trigger:** Player A plays their last card.
2. **Finish:** Complete the current trick.
3. **The Final Bell:** The player to the **Left** of Player A leads **ONE FINAL TRICK**.
   - Everyone plays one card. Winner takes it.
4. **Cleanup:** All cards remaining in hands are discarded to the Crate (No points).

------

## 6. ⚡ FACTION TALENTS



*Talents trigger ONLY when you **WIN A TRICK** with that Faction.*

| Color | Faction                | Talent Name               | Effect                                                       |
| ----- | ---------------------- | ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| 🔴     | **Finders-Keepers**    | **Borrow Aggressively**   | **Reveal** 1 random card from an opponent's hand. You may **discard 1 card** from your hand to force them to discard that revealed card. |
| 🟠     | **Munchies**           | **Snack Forecast**        | Look at the **Top 3** of the Snack Vault. Add **1** to your hand. Put the other 2 on the bottom. |
| 🟣     | **Time Travelers**     | **Pocket the Past**       | **Bank 1 Card** from your hand directly to your Stash (Points). Then **Draw 1**, then **Discard 1**. |
| 🟡     | **Tinkerers**          | **Quick Adjustment**      | Look at the **Top Card** of the Snack Vault. You may **Swap** 1 card from your hand with it. |
| 🔵     | **Magicians**          | **Fair Trade (Probably)** | **Take 1 Random Card** from an opponent. Then **Give them 1 Card** from your hand. |
| 🟢     | **Prognosticationers** | **Flow Correction**       | **Draw 2 Cards**. Then place **1 Card** from your hand on the **bottom** of the Snack Vault. |

------

## 7. 🏆 SCORING & VICTORY



1. Count cards in your **Collected Stash**.
2. **1 Card = 1 Victory Point**.
3. **New Round:** Shuffle everything and deal a new round. The player with the **lowest total score** leads the new round.
4. **Game Over:** Immediate victory at **50 Points**.

------

## 8. 🔥 VARIANT: THE COUNCIL ELECTION



*Recommended for experienced players.*

Instead of revealing cards from the deck, players vote to determine the Council Seats using their own hands.

1. **Oversized Deal:** Deal **+3 Cards** to every player during Setup (e.g., 12 cards for 3 players).
2. **The Campaign:** Players bid for the seats using cards from their hand.
   - **Vote 1 (The Big Cheese):** Everyone reveals 1 card from their hand. **Highest Rank wins.** Winner's suit takes Seat 1.
   - **Vote 2 (The Favorites):** Everyone reveals 1 card from their hand (Cannot play Big Cheese suit). **Highest Rank wins.** Winner's suit takes Seat 2.
   - **Vote 3 (The Wobbly Seat):** Everyone reveals 1 card from their hand (Cannot play Seat 1 or 2 suits). **LOWEST Rank wins.** Winner's suit takes Seat 3.
3. **Cleanup:** All voted cards are discarded to the Crate. Players now have the correct starting hand size (9/8/7).
