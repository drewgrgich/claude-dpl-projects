# Print-and-Play Strategy
## March 2026

---

## What to Include in PnP Download

- 66-card deck with prototype art (faction colors + rank numbers + faction symbols)
- Card backs (uniform design)
- One-page setup guide per game (Get Stuffed, Summit Scramble, Contests of Chaos)
- Watermark on every page: "Prototype art. Final illustrations by Danny Beck."
- Feedback form link

## What NOT to Include

- Danny's final art (that's the upgrade incentive)
- Event cards or Playbook cards (save for box)
- Anything that makes PnP feel "complete enough" to skip backing

## The Psychology

The PnP lets them experience the mechanics. The campaign page shows them the art.
The gap between prototype and final product IS the reason to back.

"I played this with stick figures and it was already fun. With Danny Beck's art? Take my money."

## Email Capture

Every PnP download = email collected = potential Day 1 backer notification.
Target: 8,000+ emails pre-launch.

## Feedback Loop

Include feedback form link in PnP PDF.
Playtesters who submit feedback become emotionally invested backers.
They helped shape it. Now they want to own it.
