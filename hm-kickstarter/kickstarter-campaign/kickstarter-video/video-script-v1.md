# Kickstarter Video: "The Fun Laboratory"
## 90-Second Script
## Design Play Labs

---

## THE CONCEPT

A Q-branch style laboratory where the science of fun is studied, tested, and perfected. The host (Drew) plays a slightly-mad scientist who has cracked the code on what makes card games delightful.

**Tone:** Whimsical, curious, energetic, slightly self-aware  
**Visuals:** White lab, colorful beakers (ROYGBIV), clipboards, failed experiments, breakthrough moment  
**Music:** Upbeat, quirky, building to triumphant reveal

## KEY VISUAL ELEMENTS

### The Lab Aesthetic
- White walls, stainless steel tables
- Beakers with colored liquids matching factions (ROYGBIV)
- Monitors showing "FUN METRICS" and graphs
- Clipboard with "PLAY TEST LOG"
- "DAYS WITHOUT A BAD GAME IDEA" sign (number is low)

### Props
- Lab coat (with Design Play Labs logo)
- Safety goggles (perched on head)
- The 66-card deck (hero prop)
- Danny Beck's art prints (framed, prestigious)
- "Failed" prototype cards (for the failure wall)
- Beakers, test tubes, fun science equipment

### Color Palette
- **Lab:** White, silver, clean
- **Accents:** ROYGBIV (faction colors in beakers, charts, lights)
- **Art:** Danny's vibrant, warm, fuzzy monster palette
