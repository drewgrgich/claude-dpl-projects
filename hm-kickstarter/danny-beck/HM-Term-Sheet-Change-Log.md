## Changes Made

### 1. Product Name Updated
**Section:** Title and throughout
**Change:** Updated working title from "Hamsters & Monsters: War!" to "Hamsters & Monsters: Contests of Chaos" throughout the document.

### 2. Confidentiality Provision Added
**Section:** 14 (new)
**Change:** Added mutual confidentiality obligations covering the term sheet, the Definitive Agreement, and non-public business/financial/technical information. Includes standard carve-outs (publicly available information, prior knowledge, independent development, legally compelled disclosure). Obligations survive termination or expiration for two (2) years.

### 3. Section Renumbering
**Sections:** 15, 16
**Change:** Former Section 14 (Notices and Written Acceptance) renumbered to Section 15. Former Section 15 (Non-Binding; Governing Law) renumbered to Section 16.

### 4. Late Payment Interest Added
**Section:** 6.4
**Change:** Added late payment interest provision: overdue amounts accrue interest at 1.5% per month (or the maximum rate permitted by law, if lower) from the due date until paid.

### 5. Currency Definition Added
**Section:** 2
**Change:** Added definition of "Dollars" or "$" as United States dollars. Clarifies that all amounts in the term sheet and Definitive Agreement are in U.S. dollars.

### 6. Termination for Material Breach Added
**Section:** 12
**Change:** Added mutual termination right for material breach by either party, with a thirty (30) day written cure period. Termination under this provision does not limit other remedies available to the non-breaching party.

### 7. Commission Milestone Math Corrected — First Milestone
**Section:** Exhibit A5
**Change:** Changed first milestone from 40% ($1,780) to 40% ($1,480). Corrected to reflect the accurate milestone base of $3,700 (excluding game logo at $350 and IG/TikTok stories at $500, which are paid separately).

### 8. Commission Milestone Math Corrected — Second Milestone

### 9. Commission Milestone Math Corrected — Third Milestone
**Section:** Exhibit A5
