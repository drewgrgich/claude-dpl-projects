### 7.4 IG/TikTok Story Campaign (Minimum 10)
- **Format:** Instagram Stories and/or TikTok Stories (vertical 9:16). Stories may be single-frame or multi-frame.
- **Quantity & Cadence:** Minimum **10** story placements, scheduled **1 per week** during the **10-week window leading up to launch** (week -10 through week -1).
- **Rate:** **$50 per story placement** (total **$500** for 10).
- **Schedule Lock:** Publisher and Artist will agree on a posting calendar by [__ date].
- **CTA:** Each story will include a clear call-to-action (e.g., follow the Kickstarter prelaunch page / join the email list) and a link method appropriate to the platform (e.g., link sticker, bio link, pinned link).
- **Content:** Story themes to be mutually agreed (e.g., art reveals, behind-the-scenes, quick gameplay hooks, countdown reminders). Artist retains voice/style consistent with brand integrity.
- **Approval Scope:** Publisher may request reasonable factual corrections (dates, links, pricing, campaign claims). Creative execution remains Artist-controlled.
- **Proof / Deliverable:** Within 24 hours of posting, Artist provides link (if available) plus a screenshot/video capture showing the story and posted time/date.
- **Validity:** A story counts if it is live for at least **24 hours** (or platform default) and includes the agreed CTA/link.

#### Payment for Stories
- **Deposit:** **$100** due upon schedule confirmation; applied against the $500 total.
- **Pay-per-story:** Remaining amounts due **per story** within **7 days** after each story goes live upon receipt of proof.
- **Make-good:** Missed/invalid stories must be reposted within **7 days** or refunded for that placement.
- **Rescheduling:** With at least **48 hours** notice, either party may reschedule a week without penalty.
