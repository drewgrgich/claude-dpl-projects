## 2. Card Game Kickstarter Trends & Pricing

### Pricing Insights

| Tier Type | Price Range | Notes |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| Digital/Print-and-Play | €5-11 | Low barrier, instant delivery |
| Standard Card Game | $15-25 | Core deck, basic tuck box |
| Deluxe Edition | $35-50 | Premium cards, metal coins, etc. |
| All-In/Collector | $75-150 | Exclusives, expansions, add-ons |

### Key Trends (2025-2026)

1. **IP Leverage is King**
   - Licensed properties (LOTR, Cyberpunk, Mortal Kombat) dominate
   - Existing fanbases = instant backers

2. **Digital/Print-and-Play Hybrid**
   - Lower price points for accessibility
   - Red Dungeon model: €5-9 digital files
   - Reduces manufacturing risk

3. **Stretch Goal Strategy**
   - Realistic initial goals
   - Tiered stretch goals maintain momentum
   - Exclusive cards/content for backers

4. **Multi-Game Systems Emerging**
   - Cerberus Engine (used in Mortal Kombat) - shared deck-building framework
   - Modular systems allowing expansions
   - "One deck, multiple experiences" gaining traction

## 4. Board Game Kickstarter Strategy Tips

### Timing (Data-Backed for 2026)

**Best Months:** September and October (slight edge)  
**Best Days:** Early in the week (Mon-Wed) for mid-week buzz  
**Launch Time:** High traffic hours (typically 10am-2pm EST)

### Success Factors

1. **Early Momentum is Critical**
   - Campaigns that fund early build confidence
   - Kickstarter algorithm favors early success
   - Social proof drives more pledges

2. **Realistic Goal Setting**
   - Achievable initial targets
   - Stretch goals keep momentum
   - Don't price out casual backers

3. **Email List Building**
   - Start months in advance
   - Warm audience = day-1 pledges
   - Critical for algorithm boost

4. **Visual Storytelling**
   - Kingdom Death raised $1M in 19 minutes via compelling visuals
   - Video content essential
   - Clear component showcases

5. **Tiered Rewards Strategy**
   - Entry tier: accessible price
   - Mid-tier: best value perception
   - High-tier: exclusives for superfans
   - Deluxe editions boost perceived value

### Platform Ecosystem

- **Kickstarter** = launch platform (like Amazon - functional but generic)
- **Pledge Managers** (PledgeBox, BackerKit) = post-campaign hub (like Shopify - customizable)
- **Late Pledges** = significant revenue opportunity post-campaign

## Key Takeaways for Card Game Launch

1. **IP or Unique Hook** - Licensed properties dominate, but innovative mechanics can break through
2. **Price Accessibility** - $15-25 sweet spot for standard; offer deluxe for collectors
3. **Multi-Game Potential** - Design for expandability/modularity from day one
4. **Community First** - Build email list before launch; early backers drive algorithm
5. **Hybrid Models** - Consider digital/print-and-play tiers for lower-risk entry
6. **Stretch Goals** - Realistic base goal; exciting stretch goals to maintain 30-day momentum
