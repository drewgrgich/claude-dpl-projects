## Design Principles

1. **Professional First:** Manufacturers need to trust you. Avoid "too cute" or "too indie."
2. **Science + Play:** The flask is literal; the playfulness comes through color and composition.
3. **Breathing Room:** Lots of white/negative space. Clean beats busy.
4. **Consistency:** All greens must match the logo exactly.
5. **Scalability:** SVG preferred for icons; PNG acceptable for photos/gradients.
